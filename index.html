Here are the top 10 wellness web apps based on user reviews and ratings:

  2. MyFitnessPal (https://www.myfitnesspal.com/) - This app is a comprehensive fitness tracker that helps users track their workouts, monitor their nutrition, and reach their fitness goals.

  3. Happify (https://www.happify.com/) - This app offers evidence-based mental health and wellness programs, including stress management, resilience training, and sleep improvement.

  4. Calm (https://www.calm.com/) - This is a popular mindfulness and meditation app that helps users reduce stress, improve sleep, and find inner peace.

  5. Fitbit (https://www.fitbit.com/) - This app is a comprehensive fitness tracker that helps users track their workouts, monitor their nutrition, and reach their fitness goals.

  7. Noom (https://www.noom.com/) - This app is a weight loss and wellness program that combines coaching, personalized meal plans, and habit tracking to help users achieve their health goals.

  9. MyPlate by Livestrong (https://www.livestrong.com/myplate/) - This app is a comprehensive nutrition tracker that helps users monitor their food intake and reach their health goals.

  10. Moodfit (https://www.moodfit.com/) - This app is a mental health and wellness program that helps users build resilience, improve their mood, and manage stress.
